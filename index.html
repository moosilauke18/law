About Us

Cohen Nicoleau is a Miami-based law firm focusing on banking, commercial finance, real estate, regulatory compliance, commercial litigation, and commercial transactions. With decades of experience that includes work for federal regulatory agencies and major banks, the trusted attorneys of Cohen Nicoleau have achieved exemplary results in these important legal fields for their clients.


Our Firm Members

Lewis R. Cohen

Formally general counsel for a Florida based national bank and outside general counsel for a number of community banks, Mr. Cohen has more than 35 years' experience in the areas of banking, commercial finance, regulatory compliance, commercial transactions, and commercial litigation.

Since 1990, Mr. Cohen has held the highest rating ("AV") from the prestigious Martindale-Hubbell Law Directory and is listed in the Bar Registry of Preeminent Lawyers. Mr. Cohen has been recognized for professional excellence as a "Top Lawyer" by the South Florida Legal Guide every year since 2005, and as a leader in South Florida's banking and finance community by the Daily Business Review.

Michael D. Nicoleau

Mr. Nicoleau has extensive experience in commercial litigation, banking laws, and banking regulations. He is a former senior attorney and counsel for the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation's Legal Division.

While at the FDIC, Mr. Nicoleau worked in the Special Issues Unit, where he supervised and managed the FDIC's outside counsel in complex commercial litigation. He also advised banks (and their counsel), which purchased failed banks from the FDIC, regarding litigation and regulatory related matters. Prior to the FDIC, Mr. Nicoleau practiced commercial litigation at both a prominent Washington, D.C. law firm and a national New York law firm.

Fredric I. Gottlieb

Mr. Gottlieb has more than 30 years' experience in the areas of commercial transactions, real estate transactions, and related litigation.

Mr. Gottlieb handles residential and commercial real estate transactions, from contracts to closings. He also represents a multitude of individual and corporate clients, as both plaintiffs and defendants, including, but not limited to, buyers, sellers, lenders, realtors, brokers, condominium and homeowner associations, lien holders, property management companies, and title insurance companies, for their respective transactional needs, and as their litigation counsel.
